Meet our Team

Welcome to Bean Tag! Step into a whimsical world where you become a nimble bean, leaping and bounding through obstacles in a virtual reality adventure inspired by the popular Gorilla Tag game. Experience thrilling gameplay and connect with friends in a brand new way as you explore this exciting VR universe.

Version 1.7.9

Welcome to version 1.7.9 of Bean Tag! This major update brings a refreshed forest environment and a brand new holdable cosmetic item to enhance your gameplay experience. We've also taken care of some pesky bugs, including a fix for the 'Mute Music' option and improvements to the 'Platforms' feature for moderators. Plus, we've polished the ball cosmetic for even smoother gameplay. Get ready to dive in and explore all the exciting changes!
